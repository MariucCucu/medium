Pyrpose Raises $1.1m to Develop Climate Finance Platform

Pyrpose, a groundbreaking climate finance platform based in Geneva, has announced a successful fundraising round of $1.1m. The platform connects environmentally conscious consumers with climate innovators, helping to drive down future carbon emissions.

The platform is currently in private beta. It allows users to gauge their carbon footprint, invest in climate solutions, and in turn, provide necessary working capital to SMEs dedicated to improving our planet's future. This funding round includes institutional investment from CV VC and various global business angels.

The fresh funds will be used to finalize the private beta, refine the product for its Q3 MVP launch, and accelerate the mission further. Pyrpose plans to open its seed round soon.

CEO and Co-Founder of Pyrpose, Kevin Kyer, thanked the angels and CV VC for their support, highlighting the importance of making climate finance accessible to all. David Long of CV VC also spoke about the Pyrpose platform, emphasizing its usability, transparency, and the potential for users to make a meaningful difference in the fight against climate change.

About Pyrpose

Pyrpose is a transparent climate finance platform catering to the "now generation" change makers. The platform links climate conscious consumers with climate innovators. The private beta of the platform was launched in February 2023, and the company is headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland.

About CV VC

CV VC is a global blockchain investor with hubs in Switzerland, Liechtenstein, South Africa, Germany, and Portugal. It offers seed funding and an acceleration program to global tech teams in exchange for equity or tokens. The company also provides consulting and advisory services to governments and corporations in the blockchain industry.
